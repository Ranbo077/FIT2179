Cleaned data for FIT2179 DV2: Australia’s Changing Cultural Landscape

Files:
- state_diversity_summary.csv: one row per state/territory, ready to join to map boundaries by state.
- migration_trend_australia.csv: financial-year net overseas migration for Australia.
- migration_by_state_2024_25.csv: latest state/territory net overseas migration.
- country_of_birth_top_2025.csv: top birth countries in Australia in 2025 with 1996 comparison.
- country_of_birth_trend_top8.csv: long-format population trends for the top 8 birth countries.
- overseas_born_share_trend.csv: total population, overseas-born population and overseas-born percentage over time.
- birth_country_by_state_2021_top10.csv: top 10 birth countries for each state/territory in 2021.
- language_summary_by_state_2021.csv: English vs non-English language used at home by state/territory.
- language_by_state_2021_top20.csv: top 20 non-English languages by state/territory.
- top_languages_australia_2021.csv: top 20 non-English languages nationally.

Sources:
- ABS 3409.0 Australia’s population by country of birth, 2025: 34090DO001_2025.xlsx and 34090DO002_2025.xlsx
- ABS Census of Population and Housing: Cultural diversity data summary, 2021: Cultural diversity data summary.xlsx
- ABS 3407.0 Overseas Migration, 2024-25: 34070DO001_202425.xlsx

Notes:
- Counts are ABS rounded counts. Percentages are computed from rounded counts, so small differences from published values are possible.
- For map joins, use the state column and make sure your TopoJSON state names match these values exactly.
